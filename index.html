VAI is a community for aspiring VTubers and creators to learn, collaborate, and shine—together. Through hands-on workshops, mentorship programs, and real streaming experience, students build both technical skills and creative confidence.

🌟 Featured Programs

VTuber Fundamentals

Learn the essentials of streaming, avatar setup, and audience interaction while building a strong foundation for your VTuber journey.

Voice Acting

Develop expressive performance skills and character voices through guided practice, feedback, and real-world application exercises.

Content Strategy

Understand how to plan engaging content, grow your audience, and build a consistent brand that stands out in the VTuber space.
